## Full Description (4000 characters)

**WhatsApp Message Sender - Send Messages Without Saving Contacts**

Tired of saving contacts just to send a quick WhatsApp message? WhatsApp Message Sender makes it easy to open WhatsApp chats with any phone number without cluttering your contact list.

**Key Features:**
✅ Send messages without saving contacts
✅ Quick country code selection (default: +91)
✅ Pre-fill message text (optional)
✅ Works with WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business
✅ Clean, simple Material Design interface
✅ No permissions required
✅ Fully offline - no internet needed
✅ No ads, no tracking, completely free

**How It Works:**
1. Enter the country code (default: +91 for India)
2. Enter the phone number
3. Optionally type your message
4. Tap "Send Message"
5. WhatsApp opens with the chat ready to send

**Privacy & Security:**
- No data collection
- No user tracking
- No ads
- No permissions required
- All processing happens on your device
- No data sent to servers

**Perfect For:**
- Quick one-time messages
- Business communications
- Customer support
- Delivery confirmations
- Appointment reminders
- Any situation where you don't want to save a contact

**Requirements:**
- WhatsApp must be installed on your device
- Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher

**Note:**
This app uses the official WhatsApp deep link API. We are not affiliated with WhatsApp or Meta. This is an independent utility app.

Download now and start sending WhatsApp messages without saving contacts!
