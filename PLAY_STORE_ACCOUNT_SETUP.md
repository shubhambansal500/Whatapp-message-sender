# Google Play Developer Account Setup

## ✅ Yes, You Need a Developer Account

To upload apps to the Google Play Store, you **must** have a Google Play Developer account.

## Account Requirements

### 1. One-Time Registration Fee
- **Cost**: $25 USD (one-time payment, not recurring)
- **Payment Method**: Credit card or debit card
- **Validity**: Lifetime (no annual renewal fee)

### 2. Google Account
- You need a Google account (Gmail account)
- This will be your developer account email

### 3. Developer Information
- **Personal Account**: Your name and address
- **Organization Account**: Company name, address, and contact info

## How to Create a Developer Account

### Step 1: Go to Play Console
Visit: https://play.google.com/console

### Step 2: Sign In
- Sign in with your Google account
- If you don't have one, create a Gmail account first

### Step 3: Pay Registration Fee
- Click "Create developer account"
- Pay the $25 one-time registration fee
- Complete the payment process

### Step 4: Complete Profile
- Fill in developer information:
  - Developer name (appears in Play Store)
  - Contact email
  - Phone number
  - Address (required for tax purposes)

### Step 5: Accept Terms
- Read and accept Google Play Developer Program Policies
- Accept Distribution Agreement

### Step 6: Account Verification
- Google may verify your identity
- This can take a few hours to a few days

## Account Types

### Personal Developer Account
- **Best for**: Individual developers, hobby projects
- **Requirements**: Personal name and address
- **Tax**: You'll receive tax forms if you earn money

### Organization Account
- **Best for**: Companies, businesses
- **Requirements**: Business name, business address, tax ID
- **Tax**: Business tax information required

## Important Notes

### One Account, Multiple Apps
- ✅ One developer account can publish **unlimited apps**
- ✅ You don't need to pay $25 for each app
- ✅ The $25 is a one-time fee for the account

### Account Benefits
- Upload unlimited apps
- Access to Play Console analytics
- Manage app releases and updates
- Handle user reviews and ratings
- Access to beta testing features
- Revenue tracking (if you monetize)

### Account Restrictions
- ⚠️ Account can be suspended for policy violations
- ⚠️ Suspended accounts cannot publish new apps
- ⚠️ Follow Google Play policies strictly

## Cost Breakdown

### Initial Setup
- **Developer Account**: $25 (one-time)
- **App Upload**: Free
- **App Updates**: Free
- **Total First Year**: $25

### Ongoing Costs
- **Annual Fee**: $0 (no recurring fees)
- **Per-App Fee**: $0
- **Update Fee**: $0
- **Total Ongoing**: $0

### Revenue Share (if you monetize)
- **Google Play Fee**: 15% or 30% of revenue
  - First $1M: 15%
  - After $1M: 30%
- **Only applies if you charge for apps or in-app purchases**

## Alternative Options

### If You Don't Want to Pay $25

1. **F-Droid** (Free, open source)
   - Free to publish
   - Only open source apps
   - Your app qualifies (MIT License)
   - Visit: https://f-droid.org/

2. **GitHub Releases** (Free)
   - Host APK on GitHub
   - Users download directly
   - No app store distribution

3. **Direct Distribution**
   - Share APK file directly
   - Users install manually
   - No store needed
